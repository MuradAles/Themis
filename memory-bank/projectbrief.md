# Project Brief: Themis

**Project Name:** Themis  
**Organization:** Steno  
**Project Type:** AI-Powered Demand Letter Generator  
**Project ID:** 8mKWMdtQ1jdYzU0cVQPV_1762206474285

## Core Mission

Themis is an AI-driven web application designed to streamline the creation of demand letters for law firms. The tool automates the drafting process by leveraging AI to analyze source documents and generate professional demand letters, significantly reducing the time attorneys spend on this critical litigation task.

## Primary Goals

1. **Efficiency:** Reduce time to draft demand letters by at least 50%
2. **Quality:** Generate professional, firm-standard demand letters
3. **Customization:** Support firm-specific templates and customization
4. **User Experience:** Provide intuitive interface for document management and editing
5. **Adoption:** Achieve 80% user adoption rate within first year

## Target Users

- **Primary:** Attorneys at law firms
- **Secondary:** Paralegals and Legal Assistants

## Key Value Propositions

- Automated draft generation from source documents
- Real-time collaboration and editing capabilities
- Firm-specific template management
- Professional Word document export
- AI-powered refinement and customization

## Success Metrics

- 50% reduction in draft creation time
- 80% user adoption rate (first year)
- Increased client retention and satisfaction
- Generation of new sales leads through innovative AI solutions

## Project Scope

**In Scope:**
- Document upload and management
- AI-powered letter generation
- Template creation and management
- Real-time editing and collaboration
- Word document export
- User authentication and authorization

**Out of Scope:**
- Mobile application
- Third-party legal practice management software integration
- Advanced AI features beyond draft generation and basic refinements
